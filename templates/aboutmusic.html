About the music

Music, where can we begin with music? Music has never always been a massive part of my life, in fact when I was a tadpole I hated all aspects of music and never listened to any of it ( yes really ). Music really only started changing everything in my life since my late teens or early 20's where I started exploring music games more often, hearing regular songs on the radio and I started liking a few!

I got into game soundtracks and EST's for a few years and would be my go too when I was working on a project or playing a game to keep me entertained. From this day forward I was always interested in music genre type games ( as well as lots of others ) and had to play all of them that I could find. I had an ear for music that not a massive amount of people do, in short if we are expected to hear a beat here then they better sure be one other wise its just going to sound aweful and my ears/brain can pick this up. With this ability and exploring music for years to do this day, I picked up DJ'ing and Music production both of which you can click on to find out a more in depth explanation of these, and I was loving music and it became one of my favorite things in life from hating the stuff before.
