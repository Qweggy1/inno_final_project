The real fun stuff! Music production

Since I was young and growing up into my teenage years I never actually liked music, not a single genre or song. I didn't even like game sound tracks or custom made songs from youtube. As i got closer to my adult ages i really started having an ear for music and ended up liking every genre under the sun ( to some extent ). I started playing beat songs, so for a few examples games such as Guitar Hero, Music racer, Audio surf, and my singing monsters ( of all things ) to name a few until i found Fuser which you all know about!

Eventually, I got into using DAWs after learning how to DJ. DAWs are the naming programs use for professional music production with a whole host of options to change and layouts to get your head around at first, it is extremely overwhelming if you have never seen one before. Wrong! if you can look over the 1000's of buttons and break it down section by section it becomes natural, not straight away obviously "You can't expect to succeed if you only put in the work on the days you feel like it" they are a multitude of tutorials on the web and Youtube, nobody expects you to learn everything alone, Google is your best friend and is the perfect place to start if you are interested in that sector of music making. "JUST DO IT". Know the juicy stuff, i have provided a large majority of my songs for you to view below if you want to have a listen please be my guest.
